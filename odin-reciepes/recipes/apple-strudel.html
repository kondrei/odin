Apple Strudel

Description

Use ready made phyllo pastry and homemade apple filling.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet apples -- peeled, cored and thinly sliced
  • ¼ cup golden raisins
  • ¼ cup dried currants
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 slices brown bread, crumbled
  • ½ (16 ounce) package phyllo dough
  • ¼ cup butter, melted

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).

  2. Step 2

    3. In a bowl, combine apples, raisins, currants, cinnamon, sugar and bread crumbs. Stir well.

  3. Step 3

    4. Spread several sheets of pastry generously with melted butter and lay them on atop the other on a baking sheet. Spread the fruit mixture evenly over the top sheet, then roll the sheets up to form a log shape. Brush with melted butter again.

  4. Step 4

    5. pBake in preheated oven 30 minutes, until pastry is golden brown and fruit is tender.
